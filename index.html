A story that warms every heart

Ralphy is white, fluffy, and small — but don't let that fool you. He's boldest of all! Follow Ralphy through one perfect day: a morning walk where the whole block is his, a backyard full of pure magic, treat time (the very best part!), belly rub hour, and a soft, cozy ending under the stars. ⭐

Written in joyful rhyme, this book reminds little ones — and the grown-ups reading to them — that a beautiful day starts right under your feet. 🐾
